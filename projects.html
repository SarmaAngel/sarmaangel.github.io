Projects

My Dress to Impress Database

I developed a website to showcase data from 51 points collected while playing the game Dress to Impress over 2 months.

My role was to analyze the data, implement the design, and develop a responsive user interface using HTML, JavaScript, and React.js.

This project was interesting and enjoyable, as I had to figure out how to present the data in a way that was both informative and visually appealing.

To see the full website and my code, please view the video to the left.

The Space Between

This was an interactive comic made in Unity with a group of 4, where I acted as the Narrative Lead and designed the storyline for the game.

I worked in Unity using C# to create the keypad/password mechanic, made the exploring-level sprites, animations, and tilemap using Aesprite, and I illustrated my comic panels in Procreate.

It was a fun experience to work with a group, get to write and work with the storyline, and focus on the art.

For more information about the development of the game, please view the video to the left.

Galactic Buzzkill

This was an AR puzzle platformer game made in Unity with a group of 4, where I acted as the Art Lead and Director.

I worked in Unity using C# to create gameplay mechanics such as the platform waypoints and Gravity Switch, and designed and animated character and item sprites using Procreate.

I enjoyed the shared work development experience, and one of my favorite parts was figuring out fun and unique level design for the puzzles and obstacles.

For more information about the development of the game, please view the video to the left.
